Groupwork during our
prototyping course,
working with Lo-Fi &
Hi-Fi prototypes.

My group worked towards ikea.se since it's a well known
website to the majority of the swedish population.
After carrying out some user research our goal was to get the user
to navigate through Ikeas navbar and menu. During our research
we found that most users would rather search for an item than using the
built in navigation. We focused on making the navigation shorter with reducing
the steps it takes to find what you're looking for, as well as designing the
navbar in a similar way to other furniture websites, to make the user
feel familiar with the navigation system.
