My project based on a pitch, a dating app that focuses on
connecting before juding "the cover". Looking at flaws with the current
dating apps on the market my research gave me some answers,
it's way too superficial and it's extremely hard to start a conversation with
someone based on how you look. People with low self esteem
might find it extremely daunting to put themselves out there,
since they are not confident with how they look. I wanted to create a place that
feels safe, including and warm for EVERY PERSON .
